Data Visualization: Smule App Data - Collaboration & Playback

Let's say... I want to up my game and get people to join me more and listen more.

Trends?
From the graphs, recordings that were created on the weekends seem to get more collaborations or have more playbacks. However, most of the recordings were also created on the weekends. We can probably write this off as, the days don't matter.

Just for fun: If I want more playbacks with a specific user, maybe I can target the days that tend to have more playbacks. For example, I get more playbacks for recordings I created with Pannekoek on Tuesday or Thursday, unlike others where they follow the same overall trend. Maybe these users have their own fans that listen to their recordings on a certain day, so in order to target that +1 playback I will have to sing on a certain day with a specific user.
