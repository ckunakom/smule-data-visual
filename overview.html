Data Visualization: Smule App Data - Overview

The overview dashboard provides the basic information on my account activity:

  • the types of singing activities (solo, duet, group) and the number of recordings I have for each;
  • the types of invitations (owner vs joiner) and the number of recordings I have for each;
  • the number of of recordings over the timespan of September 2016 - March 2021.

Main Points:

  • Over 99% of my recordings are duets, meaning I sang with another person most of the time.
  • I joined others more than creating an invitation for others to join except on the weekends.
  • I was very active in 2017, both in terms of sending and joining invitations. It went downhill from there.
